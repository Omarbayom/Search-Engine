About Faculty of Engineering

Welcome to Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering at Ain Shams University, established in 1839 as the School of Operations in Bulaq, evolved through various stages, becoming the Higher Institute of Engineering in 1946 and later the Faculty of Engineering in 1950. It has expanded its departments, adopted a credit-hour system, formed global partnerships, and focuses on research addressing societal and environmental challenges.

Admission

The Faculty of Engineering at Ain Shams University warmly welcomes ambitious students ready to shape the future and excel in their fields. Here, education transcends boundaries, blending innovative teaching with hands-on experience in a supportive environment. We are committed to nurturing your talents and driving you toward success, equipping you with the skills to reach your highest potential. Apply now to embark on a transformative journey at the heart of Cairo, where your path to a distinguished future begins.
