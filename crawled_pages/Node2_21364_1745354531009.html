Fiction

Fiction is the telling of stories which are not real. More specifically, fiction is an imaginative form of narrative, one of the four basic rhetorical modes. Although the word fiction is derived from the Latin fingo, fingere, finxi, fictum, "to form, create", works of fiction need not be entirely imaginary and may include real people, places, and events. Fiction may be either written or oral. Although not all fiction is necessarily artistic, fiction is largely perceived as a form of art or entertainment.     [close] One group read a month which will be determined by a group vote - all reads will be murder/crime thrillers! Book nominations are welcome (one a month per member). Book noms are added to a poll where members decide the monthly read. If there is a tie of 2 books, we read both (optional - you can read both, one, or neither!). If there is a tie of more than 2 books, we will do a sudden death poll between all of the tied books. No self promotions please.
