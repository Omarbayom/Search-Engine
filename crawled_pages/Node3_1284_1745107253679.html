Frequently asked questions

How does the Zyte affiliate program work?

The Zyte affiliate program allows you to earn commissions by promoting our web scraping solutions. After signing up, you’ll receive a unique referral link to share. You’ll earn commissions for every customer who signs up through your link.

Who is eligible to join the affiliate program?

Anyone with a relevant audience interested in web scraping, including bloggers, content creators, and social media influencers, can apply to become a Zyte affiliate.

How do I earn commissions in this program?

You earn a commission every time someone signs up for Zyte using your unique referral link and becomes a paid user of the product. The commission rate is competitive, rewarding you for your efforts in promoting our services.

When will I receive my commission payments?

Commissions are paid monthly on the 15th of each month, allowing you to regularly monitor your earnings.

What is the minimum payout threshold?

The minimum payout amount is $150. Once your earnings reach this threshold, you will receive your payment.

How long do cookies last in the affiliate program?

The cookie duration is 60 days, meaning you’ll receive credit for any sales made by customers who click your referral link within that time frame.

What are the do’s of the program?

• Share your unique referral link on your platforms.


• Utilize the marketing materials provided by Zyte to enhance your promotions.


• Stay updated with program guidelines to maximize your success.

What are the don’ts of the program?

• Do not place Zyte ads on online auction platforms like eBay or Amazon.


• Avoid offering discounts or coupon codes for Zyte services unless you have explicit permission.


• Refrain from advertising Zyte through any Pay-Per-Click advertisement systems without prior consent.

How can I contact the affiliate team?

For any questions or support, feel free to reach out to our dedicated affiliate team at affiliate@zyte.com
