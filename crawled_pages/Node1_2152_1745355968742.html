Overview

Our Vision

Connecting the Past, Shaping the Future: A Legacy of Excellence

The Alumni Unit at the Faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University, serves as a vital bridge between our esteemed graduates and the institution that has shaped their professional journeys. Each year, we proudly welcome over 2,000 new graduates every year  into our ever-growing network of alumni. Our graduates are renowned for their exceptional technical skills, innovative thinking, and leadership qualities, making significant contributions to various industries both locally and globally.

The Faculty of Engineering at Ain Shams University boasts a rich history of producing high-caliber engineers who have excelled in their respective fields. Our alumni network is a testament to our commitment to academic excellence and professional development. They continue to uphold the university’s reputation by making impactful contributions across a wide range of sectors, including engineering, technology, academia, and government.

Our Mission

The Alumni Unit is dedicated to fostering a strong and supportive community among our graduates. We aim to: 

  • Maintain and strengthen connections between alumni and the Faculty of Engineering. 
  • Provide a platform for professional networking, collaboration, and career development. 
  • Celebrate the achievements and contributions of our alumni. 
  • Support lifelong learning and continuous professional growth through various programs and initiatives. 

Join Us,

Whether you are a recent graduate or experienced professional, the Alumni Unit at the Faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University, invites you to stay connected and be an active part of our vibrant alumni community. Together, we continue to build on our legacy of excellence and drive positive change in the world of engineering and beyond.

Through the Alumni Unit, graduates have access to exclusive job offers, opportunities, and professional development resources as well as events. Additionally, the sector conducts and manages regular surveys to gather feedback from alumni and fresh graduates, helping to enhance the quality of education and services provided by the Faculty.

We are pleased to introduce our new Alumni portal:

  • Alumni Portal: Alumni Page.
  • If you are not registered at Alumni Portal through, Alumni Registration: Register here.
  • If you already registered, Please log in via Alumni Login: Login here.

The new portal provides access to:

  • Request Certificates
  • Job Portal with exclusive job offers from companies to ASUENG graduates
  • ASU Alumni Benefits Card,  Offered from Ain Shams University Unit
  • Faculty News

Upon registration, you will find a one-time Alumni Survey to hear back from you and help us in our continuous evaluation and pursuit of educational excellence. 

To participate in Alumni Survey

  • Go to link: https://eng.asu.edu.eg/home/certificates/request/verify.
  • Please use your personal active e-mail in registration (Do not use faculty domain email).
  • Fill in your info and survey and when you reach certificate request just close your browser. 

For any inquiries and joining our database, please contact us at: alumni.unit@eng.asu.edu.eg.
