Admission Undergraduate

Admission Requirements

Steps for submitting documents for new students for the academic year 2024/2025:

This announcement is for all new students at the college. The first step in your university life is opening your personal file.

Each student should prepare the following documents:

  • Final nomination card (not preliminary notices).
  • Original birth certificate (national ID) + 3 copies.
  • Original high school certificate (GCSE) + 3 copies.
  • 10 personal photos (4×6) with the student's full name written on the back + medical examination report.
  • 2 copies of the national ID card.
  • Form (3,2) showing military service status from the recruitment administration (for males).
  • Military ID card (6 جند) (a military document in Egypt issued to male students upon reaching 19 years of age to defer military service during their studies) for those born before 2006. If unavailable, bring a form (7 جند) from the relevant department with a specific stamp.
  • Athletic incentive if applicable.
  • Hepatitis C virus analysis form.
  • Personal photo uploaded to the student's personal page should be (4×6).

Students should register on the faculty website at the following link and fill out the student data and upload copies of the aforementioned documents:

  • https://eng.asu.edu.eg/home/applyto/undergraduate

This can be done from anywhere or in the college's computer labs located in the main building.

Original copies of these documents should be submitted in an envelope to Student Affairs at the college with the student's details (name/address/phone/total grade) written clearly and prominently.
