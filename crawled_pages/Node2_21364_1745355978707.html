International Mobility

Overview

International Mobility at Faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University is dedicated to enhancing global engagement and fostering international collaboration. It supports the mobility of faculty staff, students, and visitors, contributing to a rich and diverse academic environment. From 2021 to 2024, the Faculty facilitated the mobility of over 1,100 staff and students, fostering a vibrant exchange of knowledge, culture, and expertise.

Student Mobility and Exchange Programs

Student Mobility has been remarkable, offering students opportunities to participate in international workshops, training programs, competitions, and study abroad. This expansion underscores the faculty’s dedication to enriching students' educational journeys and preparing them for a globalized workforce.

Visitor Mobility

Visiting Professors

Our faculty has welcomed visiting professors from a diverse range of countries, fostering international collaboration and academic exchange. The map below highlights the nations and institutions that have contributed to enriching our academic environment.

 

The faculty has experienced remarkable growth in the number of visiting professors, increasing more than 22 times between 2021 and 2024. This expansion has enabled us to host a wide array of workshops and academic exchanges, underscoring our commitment to fostering international collaboration. Through these efforts, we have strengthened partnerships with esteemed universities and institutions worldwide, contributing to the professional development of our faculty and enriching our academic community.

Visiting Students

Our faculty has experienced a remarkable growth in the number of visiting students from 2021 to 2024. This steady increase reflects our dedication to fostering international academic engagement and cultural exchange, attracting students from diverse backgrounds to participate in enriching workshops and collaborative programs.

International Events

The International Collaboration Office has had the privilege of hosting numerous significant events, welcoming delegations from prestigious institutions and esteemed embassies. These events reflect our commitment to fostering global partnerships and creating opportunities for academic and cultural exchange.
