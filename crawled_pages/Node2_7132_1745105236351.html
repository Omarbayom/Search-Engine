What's great about working at Zyte

Flexibility

As a remote company we understand and encourage flexibility. Working remote allows us more flexibility in our work schedule and location. We can work from anywhere, as long as there is a good internet connection and can set flexible hours to better suit personal needs and preferences. 

PTO

Take your time, relax, enjoy recharge. It's important to take time out. All full time Zytans are entitled to generous paid leave for the year which will give you the opportunity to switch off and replenish!

Wellness

Talk to the experts, we value mental health, take advantage of our great programs. We offer online counseling and a 24/7 Employee Assistance Programme to all Zytans so that they can talk to experts and take care of your mental health. 

L&D

Learn, develop, grow. We are here to support you and your career. We offer a learning and development platform for you to choose courses from and also specific training courses are offered throughout the year. 

Hangouts and events

We love being remote but we also like to get to know each other. We try to meet up (virtually and in-person), as it is a great opportunity to get to know your team up close and personal. 

Family Friendly

Every family is different. We accept them all. From dogs on calls to maternity/ paternity leave, we've got it all covered! 

Giving something back

A charitable donation for your time.
We appreciate the time that is spent on preparing for an interview or completing technical trials during our recruitment process. In recognition of this we will make a charitable donation on your behalf - for all candidates who complete a technical trial or presentation.We’re always trying to give back. Our Charity of choice this year is “Doctors Without Borders” which provides humanitarian medical aid across the world. They are active in over 70 countries and are best known for their projects in conflict zones and in countries affected by endemic diseases. A charity that fits so closely with our team.
